# 🌟 Welcome to CECP

### About the Club
The **Centre of Electronic Coding and Project (CECP)** is the heart of innovation at Roorkee Institute of Technology (RIT). We are a passionate community of tech enthusiasts, hardware hackers, and software developers. 

From building autonomous rovers and IoT smart devices to developing full-stack web applications, CECP is where raw ideas turn into working realities. This platform—**CECP**—is our official digital home. It is designed to bring all our core members, ongoing projects, and tech initiatives under one unified roof.
