// Who We Are

Engineering the Next Generation

We are a community of hardware enthusiasts, software developers, and engineering innovators. CECP serves as the bridge between theoretical concepts and real-world industrial applications, fostering an ecosystem of high-level R&D for engineering undergraduates at Roorkee Institute of Technology.

From embedded systems and defense prototyping to AI-powered computer vision pipelines, our members work on projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in student-led engineering. We compete at national-level hackathons, contribute to open source, and build tools that solve real industrial challenges.

Our Journey & Vision 🚀

Our Mission

To cultivate an elite environment where engineering concepts transcend academic theory. We empower RIT Roorkee students to design, test, and deploy robust hardware and software architectures.

From low-level bare-metal programming and multi-layer PCB routing to fully functional robotics and computer vision pipelines, CECP is where ambitious ideas hit the workbench and become working prototypes.

Our Vision

To establish CECP as the premier, industry-aligned student-run electronics and coding hub of Uttarakhand. We operate with a singular focus on achieving technical excellence and producing top-tier developers.

We aim to dominate technical symposiums, contribute meaningfully to global open-source projects, and graduate deeply skilled, industry-ready engineers who actually ship real products.

What We Do

Electronics & PCB

Mastering analog circuits, IC design theory, and precise multi-layer PCB layout using Altium and KiCad.

Embedded & IoT

Programming STM32, ESP32. Connecting the physical world to cloud architectures through MQTT and HTTP.

Robotics

Developing autonomous movement, PID controllers, sensor fusion, and mechanical integration.

Full-Stack & AI

Writing efficient backend APIs and integrating ML pipelines using YOLO, OpenCV, and PyTorch.
